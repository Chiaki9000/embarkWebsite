This is Embark

Sustainability

We're an independent, student-centred not-for-profit, based out of Simon Fraser University, creating space for transformation by catalyzing student sustainability journeys that centre justice, equity, decolonization, diversity and inclusion.

We do this by engaging students in programs, events, and other initiatives that spark dialogue and cultivate action to address systemic challenges within our climate and food systems.

Become An Embarker

Embark Sustainability members include all students enrolled in an SFU undergraduate or graduate program—if you're enrolled, you're a member with full access to all Embark Sustainability has to offer!
